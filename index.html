Who We Are

A Legacy of Trust & Excellence in Recruitment

Since 1998, Deshakthee Lanka Agencies has been at the forefront of connecting Sri Lankan talent with global opportunities. As a Private Limited Company registered under Companies Act No 07 of 2007, we have built an unparalleled reputation for reliability and quality.

Our dedicated team excels in identifying, selecting, recruiting, and nurturing the best candidates across all skill levels — from domestic workers to highly skilled professionals.
