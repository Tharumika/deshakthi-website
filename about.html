Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

"Our continual team effort is to gain the highest credibility in providing reliable Sri Lankan Human Resource mingled with extremely quality services that exceed the expectation of our customers around the globe."

Our Mission

"Deshakthee Lanka wishes to build a long term relationship with our foreign principle agents and employers linking with a strong bondage with our employees and to provide exceptional services by pursuing business through innovation and advanced technology."

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to a trusted industry leader.

1998

Deshakthee Lanka Agencies was founded with a vision to connect Sri Lankan talent with global opportunities.

2007

Registered as a Private Limited Company under Companies Act No 07 of 2007, marking a new era of growth.

2012

Received the Excellence Award from the Sri Lanka Bureau of Foreign Employment for outstanding service.

2014

Won a second Excellence Award from SLBFE, reaffirming our position as an industry leader.

Present

Operating with 4 branch offices, a certified training center, and serving clients across 5+ countries worldwide (Middle East, Singapore, Malaysia, Cyprus, Maldives).

Our Strength

What makes Deshakthee Lanka a trusted name in recruitment.

We proudly award NVQ Level Certificates and offer Housekeeping Training Courses, ensuring candidates are equipped with recognized qualifications. Our training is trustworthy, standardized, and pending Bureau authorization. Enrollment is currently open through our institution, Deshakthee Foreign Employment Training Center, located in Kalmunai, Trincomalee, Colombo 03, and Kurunegala.

Deshakthee Lanka's prime goal is to enhance the Manpower Supply Trade by recruiting suitably qualified individuals from Sri Lanka's surplus labor market. Backed by a robust network of partnerships in a highly competitive market, we have established ourselves as a trusted and friendly name in the hearts of people wherever we operate.

Despite the fact of the geographical differences we reach first and fast. We have entered into contracts to improve our main areas of business activities with our employers, Foreign Principle Agents and focus our special attention towards the following areas. We keep ready to supply Domestic Workers to Middle East, Singapore, Malaysia, Cyprus & Maldives.

Our Plan

Building the future of human resource supply.

Human Resource is the most valuable Resource in the world. So that we manage it well. Further, we wish to improve our supply chain in the categories of skilled and unskilled workers to Middle East and Other Countries.
